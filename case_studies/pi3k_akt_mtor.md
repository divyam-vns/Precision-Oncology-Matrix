
# 🧬 Case Study 2: PI3K/AKT/mTOR Pathway Dysregulation

## Key Alterations
- PTEN loss
- AKT1 E17K activating mutation
- PI3K amplification

---

## Biological Effect
Loss of tumor suppressor PTEN removes inhibition on PI3K signaling:

Growth Factor → PI3K → AKT → mTOR → survival

---

## Therapeutic Targeting
- Capivasertib (AKT inhibitor)
- Alpelisib (PI3K inhibitor)

---

## Resistance Mechanisms
- Feedback activation of MAPK pathway
- mTOR bypass signaling
